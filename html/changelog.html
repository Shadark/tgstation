KathrinBailey updated:

  • Allows damage that baked beanomorphs, monkeys and slimes deal to humans to be modified instead of being hardcoded values.
  • Allows different xenomorph types to deal different amounts of damage, if future coders or VV-happy admins will it.

MrDoomBringer updated:

  • Ghosts can now see when people connect or disconnect!

Qustinnus, Kryson updated:

  • Adds the griddle, a panless stove/grill for you to bake your meat products on. Nanotrasen has decided to move away from microwaving everything
  • Burgers are now made from patties, patties are grilled on the griddle, raw patties are made by using a rolling pin on a raw meatball
  • Most meat is grilled on the griddle now instead of microwaved
  • More meatballs are gained from meat sprite: new meatball, patty and sausage sprites

TemporalOroboros updated:

  • You can dig through rock and dirt with a spoon, elbow grease, and a lot of time
  • Removed legacy code that was rendered completely useless six years ago. A round of applause for oldcode everyone!

dragomagol updated:

  • kilo: added a missing length of disposals in mech bay
  • kilo: warden's disposals now enter the loop properly
  • kilo: lawyers can no longer get into the meeting room through the infirmary
